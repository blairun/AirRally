AirRally is a motion-controlled table tennis game that turns your Android phone into a paddle. Connect with nearby friends over Bluetooth and swing your devices to rally an invisible ball back and forth.

## Features

*   **Tutorial:** Learn to play in 30 seconds.
*   **Solo Rally Challenge:** Hit the ball against the wall to earn as many points as you can.
*   **Local Multiplayer:** Connect with a friend over Bluetooth. Play in Rally Mode or in a Classic 1v1 Ping Pong match.
*   **Motion Controls:** Use your phone as a paddle. Hit the ball with varied force, direction, and spin.
*   **Effects:** Visual and audio feedback for hits, misses, and wins.
*   **Achievements:** Unlock new player avatars as you reach higher levels.
*   **Localization:** 10+ languages.

## Getting Started

1.  **Install:** Download the APK file from the [Releases](https://github.com/blairun/AirRally/releases) section and install it on one or two Android devices.
2.  **Connect:** Optionally, follow on screen instructions to connect to your friend's phone over Bluetooth.
3.  **Play:** Swing away!
